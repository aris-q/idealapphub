MyJournal

Android · interactive demo

  • Timeline first. Text, photos, video and voice notes in one chronological stream — a year of trips from Hawaii to the Great Wall.
  • Compact header. Only your most-used categories stay up front; “More ▾” holds the rest, and picking one promotes it forward. Tap the magnifier to search — try hotpot, Santorini or sunset; it covers titles, notes and voice transcripts.
  • Every entry shows its privacy. A footer on each card says “On device only” or “Shared”; tap it to see when, to whom, and in which album.
  • Tap a card to open it. The detail view has Share, Compose and Edit. Try sharing a private entry with Grandma Lin — its status flips to “Shared” right on the timeline.
  • Compose with AI. Select entries on the Compose tab, pick a format, and generate a memoir, travel story or social caption.
  • Routes travel with you. Import a Google Maps itinerary and keep the route map beside the memories it produced.
  • Private by default. Everything stays on-device and encrypted. AI generation sends only the entries you explicitly select.

Everything in the frame is clickable: search, chips, the + button, entry selection, Generate, and the bottom navigation. All data is sample content.
